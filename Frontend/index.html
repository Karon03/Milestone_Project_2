What is Somnus Sleep Tracker?

Somnus Sleep Tracker uses sleep data provided by you to give you valuable insights on your sleep. We can calculate the total time you sleep including any interruptions, and calculate the approximate time you were in REM sleep. You can change your sleep data at any time, and remove any sleep data you no longer want or need. Let us help you improve your sleep and start creating better sleep habits.
