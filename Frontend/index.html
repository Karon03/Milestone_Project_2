What is Somnus Sleep Tracker?

Somnus Sleep Tracker is a free to use app that uses data provided by you to calculate valuable information about your sleep such as length and quality of sleep, as well as insights to your sleep patterns and sleep stages. Somnus Sleep Tracker will calculate this data and present it in an easy to read format within your Somnus profile. You can change your sleep data at any time, and remove any sleep data you no longer want or need. Thank you for choosing Somnus Sleep Tracker and we wish you the best of luck towards achieving your highest sleep goals.
